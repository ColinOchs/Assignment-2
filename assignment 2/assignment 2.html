Biography

I was born in Fairfax, Virginia and spent most of my youth in the northern Virginia area. When I was 20 I moved to Richmond and it was there that I decided to enlist in the United States Navy to provide a better life for my infant son. I served as an Electronics Technician aboard the USS Ashland and toured the Mediterranean and Middle East. After my service I moved back to Virginia and worked in restaurants and retail while attending college, unsure of the direction I wanted my education to go.


In 2018 I moved to Tennessee and had my second son. My older one is now in college on a cross country scholarship. After finishing my Associate's degree I decided to pursue a career in web development. I have been enjoying the experience of learning everything I can in this exciting field.
